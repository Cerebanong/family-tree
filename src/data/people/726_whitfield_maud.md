## Notes
Daughter and heir of William de Whitelaw or Whitley of Randalholme. Married William Whitfield (725) c.1410, bringing Randalholme estate into the Whitfield family. Per WikiTree Whitley-55 (abt.1395-?). Ancestress of Miles Whitfield (712). Father is William de Whitelaw / Whitley (733) of Randalholme; mother (unknown name) was a Vipont/Vetripont granddaughter, transmitting Cumberland lands originally held by Robert de Vipont (d. 1371). Mother identified 2026-04-28: Joan (Vipont) de Whitlawe (737), granddaughter of Robert de Vipont (1303-1371). The Randalholme inheritance came through Joan, not through William de Whitelaw. Per WikiTree Vipont-28.

## Interesting Facts
Through her, the Whitfields gained Randalholme (originally Raynerholme), Alston Moor lands, and Kirkhaugh parish property.
