## Notes
Married Moses Rowley 22 Apr 1652 Barnstable. Mother of Mary Rowley (ID 553). Of the Fuller family — descent from Mayflower passenger Edward Fuller via her father Matthew Fuller (per multiple sources). Mother of Mary Rowley Weeks. Parents: Capt. Matthew Fuller (ID 569) and Frances (ID 570), per Matthew's 1678 will naming 'daughter Elizabeth Rowley wife of Moses'. IMPORTANT: The previously accepted descent from Mayflower passenger Edward Fuller through Matthew Fuller was DISPROVEN by DNA testing (Fuller Big Y Project, published Summer 2022 Mayflower Descendant). Matthew Fuller was NOT Edward Fuller's son. This family does NOT have a confirmed Mayflower connection through this line.
