## Notes
Wife of William de Whitelaw / Whitley (733). Mother of Maud Whitley (726) and at least two other Whitley daughters (Maud's portion was Randalholme). Per WikiTree Vipont-28 https://www.wikitree.com/wiki/Vipont-28 : Joan was a granddaughter of Robert de Vipont (1303-1371, Vipont-25), heiress (with her sister Elizabeth, who married Thomas de Blencowe) of the Vipont/Vetripont Cumberland estates after her grandfather's 1371 death. Her marriage carried the Randalholme share into the Whitelaw/Whitley line; the next generation (Maud) carried it into the Whitfield line via her marriage to William Whitfield (725). She also had a brother Robert who died 1371 holding the manor of Johnby.

## Interesting Facts
Through Joan, the Vipont/Vetripont medieval barony of Westmorland is linked into the Whitley/Whitfield/Frost line. The Vipont line traces back to Ivo de Vieuxpont (Norman, 11th c.) and includes hereditary sheriffs of Westmorland.
