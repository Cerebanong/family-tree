## Interesting Facts
James Albert Mosher was born on April 27, 1891, in Bangor, Penobscot County, Maine, the son of Charles Henry Percy Mosher (1859-1945) and Mary A. Crowell (1860-1944). He had two older sisters, Madeline Crowell Mosher (1885-1950) and Edith Mosher. James grew up in the Bangor area and lived in Bangor Ward 6 throughout his entire life.

During World War I, James registered for the draft, though the details of any active service are not recorded in available sources. On September 2, 1916, he married Elsie E. Frost in Bangor. Elsie was the youngest daughter of John Lyman Frost and Carrie Wilder Storer. The couple raised their family in Bangor Ward 6 and had three daughters: Eleanor (who died in infancy), Mary Elizabeth, and Barbara Louise.

James Albert Mosher died on April 26, 1945, in Maine, just one day before his 54th birthday. The cause of his death is not recorded in available sources. He was buried at Mount Hope Cemetery in Bangor. Both of his parents also died in the mid-1940s: his mother Mary in January 1944 and his father Charles in May 1945, just weeks after James himself. James was Kevin Frank's great-grandfather through his daughter Mary Elizabeth, who married Clifton Eugene Whitney.
