## Notes
Husband of Joan Miller Shedden (ID 100). Married ~1966 ('wife of 41 years' as of 2007). Son of Pete and Merle Jenkins Shedden (both predeceased). Children: Richard M. Jr. (Lori), Christine R.M. (Darryl) Maynard, Jeffrey D. (Lisa) Shedden — all of Fassett, PA. Seven grandchildren: Casey, Katie, Kia, Jenny, Ashley, Cassie, Blake. Predeceased by brother Dwayne Shedden and sister Agnes Makovitch. Devoted member of Fassett and Gillett community. Member: South Creek Volunteer Fire Department, South Creek Volunteer Ambulance, Lyons Club, South Creek Rod and Gun Club, Ridgebury Sportsman Club, Fassett Grange, PA Auctioneers Assoc., National Auctioneers Assoc. Fallen Firefighters service held at funeral. Funeral at Olthof Funeral Home, Elmira, NY, Dec. 14, 2007. Rev. Donald Rockwell and Rev. Charles Root officiated. Joan's obituary (2010) lists Dan (Sharon) Newbury and Steve Newbury as her children not listed in Richard's obituary — likely Joan's children from a prior relationship.

## Interesting Facts
Dick Shedden was an auctioneer for over 47 years, operating Shedden's Auctioneer Service from the family farm in Fassett, PA. In 1968, together with his family, they opened Shedden's Wholesale Store. He was a devoted member of the South Creek Volunteer Fire Department, and a Fallen Firefighters service was held in his honor. He loved to travel and was an avid hunter and fisherman. His favorite time of year was Christmas.
