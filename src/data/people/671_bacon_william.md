## Notes
William Bacon of Stretton, Rutland, England. Named in a New Haven Colony court deposition of John Fletcher and others of Milford, taken 17 Oct 1661, which stated that William had only one brother, Henry Bacon, that they both lived in Stretton, Rutland; and that Nathaniel Bacon then present at New Haven was William's oldest son. Colonial Records of New Haven p 297. Wife Ann (maiden unknown). Never emigrated; died in England. WikiTree Bacon-123.
