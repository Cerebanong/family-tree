## Notes
Father of Joan (Vipont) de Whitlawe (737). Per WikiTree Vipont-28 (Joan's profile). Eldest son of Robert de Vipont (1303-1371, Vipont-25); predeceased his father by ~9 years. Married Elena (Aubeney) (739) per WikiTree Aubeney-3. Their two daughters (Joan and Elizabeth) became co-heiresses of the Vipont/Vetripont Cumberland estates after Robert's 1371 death -- the inheritance skipped Nicholas's death generation.
