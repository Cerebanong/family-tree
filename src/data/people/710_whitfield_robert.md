## Notes
Founder of the Wadhurst branch of the Whitfield family. Buried Wadhurst 2 Jun 1541. First marriage: Katherine Wenbourne (widow), three daughters. Second marriage: Agnes Giles, three sons (Robert ~1517, John ~1520, Thomas ~1524) and a daughter (Elizabeth 1522). His son John Whitfield I (708) is the second son. Established as ironmaster after migrating from Cumberland.

## Interesting Facts
Reportedly aged 88 at death — exceptional longevity for the era. Migrated from Cumberland to Sussex and became a successful ironmaster, establishing the Wadhurst Whitfields as iron magnates of the Sussex Weald. The Whitfield ironmaster tradition continued through his son Robert (the Younger) at Worth, Sussex and Rowfant House.
