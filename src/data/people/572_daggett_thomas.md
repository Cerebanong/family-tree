## Notes
Son of John Doggett and Alice Brotherton. Styled as 'Esquire' and 'gentleman.' Official in Mayhew government. Clerk of Courts, later Justice of County Courts. Died intestate 15 Sep 1691.

## Interesting Facts
Married Hannah Mayhew, the 'favorite daughter' of Governor Thomas Mayhew Sr. Held local offices in the shire town of Edgartown.
