## Notes
Founding settler of both Hartford (1636) and Norwalk (1650/51). Listed on Founders Stone in Hartford Ancient Burying Ground and Norwalk East Norwalk Historical Cemetery. Constable in Hartford 1646. Came with uncle James Olmstead on the Lyon in 1632. WikiTree Olmstead-7. Wikipedia article exists.

## Interesting Facts
Co-founder of two major Connecticut towns: Hartford and Norwalk.
