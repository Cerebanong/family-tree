## Notes
Town Clerk of Norwalk for 29 years (1678-1707 and again 1721). Town judge. Selectman 1682-1685. Married Phebe Barlow 1 May 1673 in Fairfield, CT. 5 children: James (1675), Joseph (1676), Nathan (1678), Samuel (1683), John (1692). FindAGrave #126102788.

## Interesting Facts
Served as Town Clerk of Norwalk for nearly three decades.
