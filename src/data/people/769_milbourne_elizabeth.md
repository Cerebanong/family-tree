## Notes
Daughter of Sir Walter Devereux (d.1402) of Weobley, Herefordshire and Agnes Crophill. Her father was killed at the Battle of Pilleth against Owain Glyndwr. Inscription on her tomb at Burghill confirms her parentage.

## Interesting Facts
Her father's family later became Viscounts Hereford and Earls of Essex.
