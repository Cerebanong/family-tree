# Jeremiah Howes (ID: 628)

## Vital Information
- **Born:** ~1637, At sea, en route from England to Massachusetts
- **Died:** 1708-09-09, Yarmouth, Barnstable County, Massachusetts
- **Occupation:** Prominent citizen of Yarmouth, served on church and town committees
- **Residence:** Yarmouth, Massachusetts
- **Also Known As:** Jeremiah Howe

## Family
- **Father:** Thomas Howes
- **Mother:** Mary Howes
- **Spouse:** Mary Howes (m. ~1693, Yarmouth, Massachusetts)

## Notes
Born at sea during the Howes family's passage from England to Massachusetts ~1637. First married Sarah Prence (daughter of Gov. Thomas Prence and Mary Collier) ~1656. Sarah died 31 Mar 1707. Second married Mary Daggett (603) after Oct 1693. Date conflict: some sources say the Mary Daggett marriage was ~1693, but Sarah Prence reportedly died 1707. This may indicate the Mary Daggett marriage date is wrong, or there is confusion with a son Jeremiah Howes Jr.

## Interesting Facts
