## Notes
Married John Doggett 29 Aug 1622 at Marston Moretaine, Bedfordshire, England. WikiTree Brotherton-16. Note: Earlier sources (Banks 1911) incorrectly named John's wife as 'Hepzibah Brotherton'; Gordon Remington (1997) corrected this to Alice Brotherton. The Hepzibah was their daughter, not the wife.

## Interesting Facts
The confusion between Alice (wife) and Hepzibah (daughter) persisted in genealogical literature for nearly a century before correction.
