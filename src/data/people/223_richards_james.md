## Notes
Father of William Richards (ID 191, tinman). Identified via 1851 Census (FindMyPast GBC/1851/0004652309). Occupation: Tinman. Born ~1813 in Cripplegate, Middlesex (City of London). In 1851, lived in Christchurch parish, St Saviour Southwark, with wife Elizabeth and 8 children: James (17, tinman), William (12, our ancestor), Maria (11), Christian (8), George (7), Thomas (6), Edward (5), Alice (infant). The tinsmith trade passed from father to son — both James Sr. and James Jr. listed as tinmen. 1841 census: living at Charlotte St, Great Broadwall, Christ Church, Southwark with wife Elizabeth and children James, William, Maria, Christian, and Elizabeth Pissell (b.~1799, possible mother-in-law).
