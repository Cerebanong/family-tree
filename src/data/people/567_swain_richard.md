## Notes
Baptized at Binfield, Berkshire. Emigrated ~1635 to Rowley, MA. Married (1) Basill (possibly Saunders) — marriage recorded 8 Sep 1617 Battersea, Surrey as 'Richard Swayne and Bazill Saunders'. Married (2) Jane Godfrey Bunker (widow of George Bunker) 15 Sep 1658 Hampton, NH. One of the original purchasers of Nantucket 1659. Moved to Nantucket ~1661 with sons John and Richard. WikiTree Swain-779. FindAGrave #122980113.

## Interesting Facts
