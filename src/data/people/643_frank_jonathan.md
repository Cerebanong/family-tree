## Notes
Son of Joseph D. Frank and Sue Frank. Executive VP at FX Networks, overseeing all current series and FX Productions. Oversees shows including American Horror Story, Atlanta, It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, Snowfall, Pose, Mayans M.C., and others. Joined FX in 2008 as VP of Current Programming, promoted to SVP 2011, EVP 2016. Career began 1999 at Pariah, then Mark Johnson Productions, then Caryn Mandbach Productions. Also leads FX's documentary programming group. Advisory board member, Alliance of Women Directors. Married to Meredith Milton; children Wyatt and Greer.
