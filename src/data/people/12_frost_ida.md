## Interesting Facts
Ida Ethel Frost was born on February 8, 1881, in Maine, the second child of John Lyman Frost and Carrie Wilder Storer. She grew up in the Bangor area, appearing in the 1900 census in Bangor Ward 1 as a young woman of 19 living with her parents and several siblings. By 1910, she was still in Bangor, living in Ward 6. Ida never married, but she shared her life with a close companion, Eva Louise Grant (1880-1975), who is listed alongside her on FamilySearch records.

At some point in the 1920s, Ida and Eva relocated to Cleveland, Ohio, where they lived for approximately two decades. The 1930 census places Ida in Cleveland, Cuyahoga County, and by 1940 she was residing in Cleveland Ward 15. The reasons for their move to Ohio are not documented, but Cleveland was a booming industrial city in that era, attracting many New Englanders seeking employment opportunities.

Ida returned to Maine later in life. The 1950 census records her back in Bangor, and she lived out her remaining years in the Penobscot County area. She died on January 31, 1961, in Bangor at the age of 79. She was buried at Hill Crest Cemetery in Lagrange, Maine, alongside other members of the Frost family. Eva Louise Grant survived her by fourteen years, passing away in 1975.
