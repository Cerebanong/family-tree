## Sources

## Notes
Early settler of Bangor, arriving 1772. Soldier in the French War (1757). Ancestor and founder of the Bangor branch of the Hathorn family. Siblings include John, Micah, Solomon Hathorn. Children include Ashbel, David, Elizabeth, Deliverance, Silas Jr., Mary, Hannah.

## Interesting Facts
Pioneer settler of Bangor, Maine, arriving in 1772 when it was still wilderness.
