## Notes
WikiTree Lynde-16. Born Jan 1593/4 Dunstable, Bedfordshire. Settled Charlestown 1634. Freeman 4 Mar 1634/5. Deputy to General Court 8 times (1636-1652). Selectman 14 years. Deacon of the church. Married three times: (1) unknown first wife (possibly Hannah, buried Dunstable 11 Dec 1631) — mother of Ensign Thomas Lynde and Mary Lynde; (2) Margaret (Martin/Hudson) Jordan, widow of Henry Jordan, m. 27 Nov 1632 All Hallows Bread Street, London — mother of John, Joseph, Sarah, Hannah, Samuel, Henry; (3) possibly a third wife after Margaret's death in 1662.
