The von Olnhausen family were originally knights (Heinz von Otting zu Olnhausen), later settled in Nordheim by 1661 when Johannes von Olnhausen (1640–1698) became town judge and blacksmith. Johann Conrad von Ohlhausen (1674–1757) served as judge and mayor of Nordheim.
