## Notes
Father of John Hynckleye I (728). Will dated 1463/64; left two gardens to his sons on condition they glaze the long window on the north head of Tenham Church. The chancel is still known as 'The Hinkley Chancel'. Wife Jane. Son John Henkele subsequently made his own 1484 will to wife Marjorie and sons John, Robert and Thomas. Hasted's 'History and Topographical Survey of Kent' vol 6 (Tenham parish entry) is the secondary anchor. Geni profiles cluster around dates b. 1375-1410 -- b. ~1410 used here to maintain plausible chronology with son's birth ~1455. Parents added 2026-04-28 per Geni Hinckley/Hinclyne pedigree: father John Hinckley/Hinclyne (~1350, ID 734) and grandfather John Hinckley/Hinclyne of Lenham (~1300, ID 735, the John de Henclyne assessed for the 1346 Black Prince Knighting Aid).

## Interesting Facts
