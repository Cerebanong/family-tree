## Notes
First appears in 1653 land division of Edgartown. Granted land 'near the pines in the middle of the island' 1655. Also bought land in Falmouth. Died between Dec 1688 and Aug 1689 (his widow Mary sold land and he is referenced as deceased). First wife name unknown (died before 1658). Married (2) Mary Lynde (widow of John Butler, b. ~1629 Dunstable, Bedfordshire, England). No will or probate found. WikiTree Weeks-291.
