# Thomas Lynde (ID: 644)

## Vital Information
- **Born:** 1615-02-07, Dunstable, Bedfordshire, England
- **Died:** 1693-10-15, Malden, Middlesex County, Massachusetts
- **Age at Death:** 78
- **Occupation:** Ensign of militia
- **Residence:** Charlestown then Malden, Massachusetts
- **Military:** Ensign of local militia company
- **Immigration:** Arrived 1634 on the Griffin with father Deacon Thomas Lynde
- **Also Known As:** Ensign Thomas Lynde

## Family
- **Father:** Thomas Lynde
- **Mother:** Hannah Lynde
- **Spouse:** Elizabeth Lynde (m. ~1647, Charlestown, Massachusetts)
- **Siblings:** Mary Butler

## Notes
Eldest son of Deacon Thomas Lynde and his first wife Hannah. Owned land in Charlestown and homestead in Malden (later Melrose). Not to be confused with his father — the father was the Deacon, the son was the Ensign.
