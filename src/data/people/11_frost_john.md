## Interesting Facts
John Andrew Frost was born around 1879, the first child of John Lyman Frost and Carrie Wilder Storer. His parents had married in June 1878 in the Bradford-Milo area of Piscataquis County, Maine, and were living in the Orneville Township area at the time of his birth. Tragically, John Andrew died in 1882 at approximately three years of age. The cause of his death is not recorded in surviving documents, though childhood mortality was common in rural Maine during this era, with diseases such as diphtheria, scarlet fever, and whooping cough claiming many young lives.

He was buried at Hill Crest Cemetery in Lagrange, Penobscot County, Maine, where many members of the Frost family would also be laid to rest over the following decades. John Andrew was the eldest of seven children; his younger siblings Ida, Edith, Rychie, Lewis, Eva, and Elsie all survived to adulthood. His early death is a poignant reminder of the fragility of life in 19th-century rural New England.
