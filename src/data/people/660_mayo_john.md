# John Mayo (ID: 660)

## Vital Information
- **Born:** 1597-10-16, England (probably Farthinghoe, Northamptonshire)
- **Died:** 1676-05, Yarmouth, Barnstable County, Massachusetts
- **Age at Death:** ~78
- **Occupation:** Puritan minister; teaching elder Barnstable; pastor Eastham (1646-1655); first pastor Second (Old North) Church, Boston (1655-1673)
- **Residence:** Barnstable, Eastham, then Boston; died at son Samuel's home in Yarmouth
- **Immigration:** Arrived New England 1638
- **Education:** Magdalen College, Oxford
- **Also Known As:** Rev. John Mayo, John Maio

## Notes
Baptized 16 Oct 1597 as 'John Maio' (probably Farthinghoe, Northamptonshire). Educated at Magdalen College, Oxford. Married Tamasin Brike ~1618 (possibly Leiden, Holland — Dutch Reformed Church record disputed). Came to Boston 1638, Barnstable 1639 as teaching elder, Eastham 1646 as pastor, Second Church Boston 1655. Retired 1673. Died May 1676. Children: Hannah, Samuel, John, Nathaniel, Elizabeth. Wikipedia 'John Mayo (minister)'; WikiTree Mayo-13.

## Interesting Facts
First pastor of Boston's Second Church (Old North Church), predecessor of the Revolutionary-era church.
