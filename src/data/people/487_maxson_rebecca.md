## Sources

## Notes
Married Richard Maxson 2 Jan 1627/28 in London. After Richard's death in 1643, married John Harndell. Some sources claim she was Rebecca Marbury, daughter of Rev. Francis Marbury (father of Anne Hutchinson), but this connection is DISPUTED. Chamberlain and Clarenbach (1990) corrected earlier errors.

## Interesting Facts
Escaped the 1643 Pelham Bay massacre with children John and Rebecca in a small open boat back to Rhode Island.
