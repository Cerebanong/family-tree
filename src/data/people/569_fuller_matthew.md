## Notes
First regular physician at Barnstable. Will dated 25 Jul 1678, proved 30 Oct 1678 — names wife Frances and daughter Elizabeth Rowley. Estate appraised at £1,667 4s 6d. FindAGrave #34104400. WikiTree Fuller-117. IMPORTANT: Previously believed to be son of Mayflower passenger Edward Fuller, but this was DISPROVEN by the Fuller Big Y DNA Project (published Summer 2022 Mayflower Descendant). The General Society of Mayflower Descendants closed this lineage. A Summer 2024 article proposed Matthew was baptized 24 Feb 1613 at Redenhall as son of John Fuller (nephew of Robert Fuller, the father of Edward Fuller).
