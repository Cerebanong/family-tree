## Notes
Eldest son and first born child of James Olmstead and Hannah Coleman, born 1730 on the family farm on Olmstead Hill Road, Wilton, Fairfield County, CT. Married (1) Sarah Trowbridge 1754; (2) Sarah Patchen Jan 1789 (widow of Elias Morehouse from Ridgefield). Will dated 15 Feb 1805, proved 2 Nov 1807 in Norwalk.

## Interesting Facts
Lived and died on the Olmstead family farm on Olmstead Hill Road in Wilton. Had 7 children with Sarah Trowbridge including our James (b. 1755).
