## Notes
Governor Thomas Mayhew the Elder. Baptized 1 Apr 1593 Tisbury, Wiltshire, son of Mathew Mayhew and Alice Barter. Established the first European settlement on Martha's Vineyard, Nantucket, and adjacent islands in 1642. Purchased proprietorship of the islands. Married (1) unknown, (2) Jane Gallion/Galland. WikiTree Mayhew-74. Banks Vol. III pp. 298-328.

## Interesting Facts
Governed Martha's Vineyard for 40 years. His son Thomas Mayhew Jr was a missionary to the Wampanoag who was lost at sea in 1657.
