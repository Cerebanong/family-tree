## Notes
Son of Thomas Daggett (1630-1691) and Hannah Mayhew (1635-). Hannah Mayhew was the 'favorite daughter' of Governor Thomas Mayhew of Martha's Vineyard. Married Hannah Norton ~1686. Will dated 10 Sep 1737, proved 14 Feb 1737/38. Children: Brotherton (1686), Martha (1688), Jacob (1690), Hepzibah (1692), Deborah (1694). Parents confirmed: Thomas Daggett (572) and Hannah Mayhew (573). Banks Vol. III pp. 126-145.
