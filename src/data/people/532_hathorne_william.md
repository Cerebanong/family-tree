## Notes
WikiTree Hathorne-14. Yeoman of Binfield, Berkshire. Will dated 18 May 1650, proved 2 May 1651 by widow Sara. Left lands and tenements to son Robert on condition Robert give eldest son William £100. Father of Major William Hathorne (1607-1681) who emigrated to Salem, MA; John Hathorne (ID 520) who settled in Lynn, MA; Robert Hathorne who stayed in England; and Elizabeth (Davenport).

## Interesting Facts
Father of two sons who took very different paths: William emigrated to become a leading Salem magistrate, while Robert stayed in Binfield and inherited the family lands.
