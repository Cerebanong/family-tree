# Samuel Hinckley (ID: 667)

## Vital Information

- **Died:** 1662-10, Barnstable, Barnstable County, Massachusetts

- **Occupation:** Planter; later Barnstable settler

## Notes
Baptized 25 May 1589 Harrietsham, Kent, son of Robert Hinckley (~1537-1606) of Harrietsham. Married Sarah Soole 7 May 1617 Hawkhurst, Kent. A follower of Nonconformist minister John Lothropp; emigrated on Hercules 1635 from Tenterden, Kent; settled Scituate MA, then moved to Barnstable with Lothropp's congregation 1639. Died 'the end of October 1662' Barnstable. Father of Gov. Thomas Hinckley (658). WikiTree Hinckley-25; Geni 6000000002545933782; FindAGrave #260746194.
