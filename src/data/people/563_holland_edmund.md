## Notes
Son of Thomas Holland, 2nd Earl of Kent and Alice FitzAlan. Eleanor de Holland (wife of James Tuchet, 5th Baron Audley) was his illegitimate daughter. He married Lucia Visconti in 1407 but had no legitimate issue; the earldom became extinct at his death.

## Interesting Facts
