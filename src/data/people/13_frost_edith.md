## Interesting Facts
Edith I. Frost was born on November 22, 1882, in Maine, the third child of John Lyman Frost and Carrie Wilder Storer. Her middle initial 'I' appears consistently in FamilySearch records, though earlier family documents recorded her middle name as 'Izola.' She grew up in the Bangor area, appearing in the 1900 census in Bangor Ward 1 with her parents and siblings.

On April 14, 1906, Edith married Frank Albert Wilder (1882-1944) in Maine. Frank was originally from Greene, Androscoggin County, the son of Lewis Horton Wilder and Abbie Jane Sprague. After their marriage, the couple settled in Milo, a small town in Piscataquis County, where they would remain throughout their married life. Census records from 1910 through 1940 consistently place them in Milo. The couple had no children.

Frank Albert Wilder died on January 27, 1944, and Edith survived him by nearly four years. She died on December 29, 1947, in Maine, at the age of 65. She was buried in Penobscot County. Edith's long residence in Milo connected her to the Piscataquis County community where her parents had also lived during their early married years in neighboring Orneville Township.
