## Notes
Father of Miles Whitfield (712) of Newtonbury. Per the 1574 Whitfield pedigree compiled by John Whitfield of Skelgill and the 1575 Herald's visitation by William Dethicke (then York Herald, later Garter King of Arms), Richard had four sons: Robert, John, Myles (our 712), and Thomas. Of the Randalholme branch of the Whitfield family — descended from William Whitfield who acquired Randalholme c.1397/1410 through marriage to Maud Whitley.
