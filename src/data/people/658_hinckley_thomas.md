## Notes
Baptized 19 Mar 1618 at Hawkhurst, Kent, England (John Lothropp's church). Parents Samuel Hinckley and Sarah Soole were followers of the Nonconformist minister John Lothropp. Settled first Scituate then Barnstable. Married (1) Mary Richards 4 Dec 1641 Barnstable; she died 24 Jun 1659. Married (2) Mary (widow of Nathaniel Glover of Dorchester) 16 Mar 1660. Became Governor of Plymouth Colony upon Winslow's death 1680; served until Plymouth was merged into Massachusetts Bay in 1692. FS PID / Geni 6000000003484308787; WikiTree Hinckley-53. FindAGrave #6798664.

## Interesting Facts
Last Governor of Plymouth Colony before it was merged with Massachusetts Bay Colony in 1692.
