## Notes
Baptized 15 Apr 1596 Pitminster, Somerset, son of Thomas Richards the elder (~1571-~1620). First six children baptized at Pitminster 1620-1632. Sailed on Hopewell from Weymouth, England to New England 8 May 1635. Settled Weymouth, MA by ~1639; owned a mill in common with Henry Waltham. Selectman of Weymouth 1643. Will dated 17 Dec 1650; inventory taken 25 Jan 1650/1 valued at £1300 17s 11d including £200 real estate. Daughter Mary Richards became first wife of Gov. Thomas Hinckley.
