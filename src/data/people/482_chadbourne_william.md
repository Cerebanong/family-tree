# William Chadbourne (ID: 482)

## Vital Information
- **Born:** 1582-03-30, Tamworth, Staffordshire, England
- **Died:** ~1652, Kittery, York, Maine
- **Age at Death:** ~70
- **Occupation:** Millwright
- **Residence:** Kittery/Berwick, York, Maine
- **Immigration:** Arrived New England aboard the Pied Cow on 8 Jul 1634 with James Wall and John Goddard, under contract with Capt. John Mason of London's Laconia Company to build mills in Berwick.
- **Also Known As:** Chadburne

## Family
- **Father:** Robert Chadbourne
- **Mother:** Margaret Dooley
- **Spouse:** Elizabeth Sparry (m. 1609-10-08, Tamworth, Staffordshire, England)
  - **Children:** Patience Chadbourn

## Notes
FS PID LHVM-B87. Baptized at Church of St Editha, Tamworth. Built first water-powered saw mill and grist mill in New England at Asbenbedick Falls (Great Works River) on 22 Jul 1634. First signer of the Kittery Act of Submission, 16 Nov 1652. Piscataqua Pioneer.
