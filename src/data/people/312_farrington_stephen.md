## Notes
FamilySearch PID: M841-FYN. Buried Medway Cemetery, Medway, ME. Stephen was born in Lovell (Oxford County, western Maine) in 1825 to John Farrington (of Chelsea, MA origins) and died in Medway (Penobscot County, north-central Maine) in 1883 at age 57. The move from Lovell to Medway crossed the state; Medway was a major lumber center at the confluence of the East and West branches of the Penobscot River ("Nicatou, or the forks"). Its sawmills, active from the 1820s, were likely the draw. Medway was incorporated as a town in 1875, a few years before Stephen's death. Married Abigail H. Archer of Charlotte, Washington County, Maine; she outlived him by ~20 years, dying in Lincoln, ME c. 1903.
