Rychie Elba Frost was born on June 9, 1885, in Lagrange, Penobscot County, Maine, the fourth child of John Lyman Frost and Carrie Wilder Storer. She was buried at Hill Crest Cemetery in Lagrange alongside her parents and siblings.
