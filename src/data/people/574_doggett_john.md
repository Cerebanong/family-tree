## Notes
Immigrant ancestor. Sailed with Governor John Winthrop in 1630 from England to Salem, MA. Listed on the Watertown Founders Monument. Settled first in Watertown, moved to Rehoboth by 1646, to Martha's Vineyard by 1651. WikiTree Doggett-55. FindAGrave #34356756.

## Interesting Facts
One of the original settlers of Watertown, MA. His descendants spelled the name both Doggett and Daggett.
