# Thomas Cheney (ID: 488)

## Vital Information
- **Born:** ~1633, Roxbury, Suffolk, Massachusetts Bay Colony
- **Died:** ~1694, Cambridge, Massachusetts Bay Colony
- **Age at Death:** ~61
- **Residence:** Brighton (then Cambridge), Massachusetts
- **Military:** Served in Capt. Johnson's Roxbury company, marched from Boston 6 Jul 1675 for Mt. Hope during King Philip's War.

## Family
- **Father:** William Cheney
- **Mother:** Margaret Cheney
- **Spouse:** Jane Atkinson (m. 1655-01-11, Roxbury, Suffolk, Massachusetts Bay Colony)
  - **Children:** Hannah Butler

## Sources
Cheney Genealogy by Charles Henry Pope (1897); Geni Thomas-Cheney-Sr

## Notes
Will dated 23 Oct 1693, proved 4 Mar 1694/5. Children include: Margaret (1656), Thomas, Mehitable, John (1662), William, Mary, Jane, Joseph, Hannah (1689), Benjamin, Ebenezer. 13 Apr 1684, children Thomas, William and John 'owned the covenant' in the Roxbury church.

## Interesting Facts
King Philip's War veteran (1675).
