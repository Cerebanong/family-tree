## Notes
First wife of Richard Swain. Known from Bishop's transcript of son John's 1634 baptism at Easthamstead as 'Basill'. A marriage of 'Richard Swayne and Bazill Saunders' at Battersea, Surrey 8 Sep 1617 is almost certainly this couple. 'Bazil' was an unusual woman's name — some researchers suggest it is a nickname for Elizabeth. Died before Richard's second marriage 15 Sep 1658.
