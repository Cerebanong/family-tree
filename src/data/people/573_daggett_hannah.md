## Notes
Daughter of Governor Thomas Mayhew Sr and his second wife Jane Gallion. Described as the 'favorite daughter' of Governor Mayhew. Married Thomas Daggett ~1657.

## Interesting Facts
Daughter of the Governor who established the first European settlement on Martha's Vineyard. Survived her husband by over 30 years.
