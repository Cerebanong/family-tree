## Notes
Daughter of John and Elizabeth (Malinoski) Bialy. Last surviving of six children. Predeceased by husband John K. Coughlin (d. 1971). Survived by daughter Anne M. Coughlin (Elmira), son Dennis F. Coughlin (San Diego, CA), sons/daughters-in-law John 'Jack' M. and Alice Coughlin (Binghamton), William J. and Patricia Coughlin (Perkiomenville, PA). Grandchildren: Sean Coughlin, Erin (John) Precopio, Matthew (Danielle) Coughlin, Mary (Brian) Tizio, Eileen (T.J.) Kroboth. Great grandchildren: Ashley, Emma, Liam, Grace, Vincent, Bevin. Founding member of St. Joseph's Hospital Auxiliary, volunteered 52 years, chairman of 'Busy Hands'. Long-time communicant of St. Patrick's Church. Funeral at McInerny Funeral Home, Elmira. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Patrick's Church May 26, 2012. Interment May 29, 2012 at St. Peter & Paul's Cemetery.

## Interesting Facts
Helen was a founding member of St. Joseph's Hospital Auxiliary in Elmira, NY, where she volunteered for 52 years, much of it in the gift shop. She served on the Auxiliary board and was chairman of 'Busy Hands.' She was a long-time communicant of St. Patrick's Church and a member of the Ladies of Charity. She was the last surviving of John and Elizabeth Bialy's six children.
