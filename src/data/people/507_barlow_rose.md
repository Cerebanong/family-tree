Second married Thomas Barlow ~1650. FindAGrave #130507740. Parents confirmed: Thomas Sherwood (1593-1655) + Alice Tiler (~1587). Arrived Boston April 1634 on the Francis as a child. Rose married (1) Thomas Rumbell of Stratford, (2) Thomas Barlow ~1650, (3) Edward Nash ~1673 of Norwalk. WikiTree Sherwood-91.
