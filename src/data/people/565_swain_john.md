## Notes
Baptized 5 Oct 1634 at Easthamstead (now Bracknell), Berkshire, England. Son of Richard Swain and Basill (possibly Saunders). Married Mary Weare 15 Nov 1660 at Hampton, NH. Children: John Jr (1664, first white male child born at Nantucket), Stephen (1666), Sarah (1670), Joseph (1673), Benjamin (1679). WikiTree Swain-12.

## Interesting Facts
His son John Jr was reportedly the first white male child born on Nantucket Island.
