## Interesting Facts
Elsie E. Frost was born on June 2, 1894, in Bangor, Penobscot County, Maine, the youngest child of John Lyman Frost and Carrie Wilder Storer. She was Kevin Frank's great-grandmother, making her a key figure in the direct maternal line. Elsie grew up in Bangor, appearing in the 1900 census in Ward 1 as a child of six and in the 1910 census in Ward 6 as a teenager of 15.

On September 2, 1916, Elsie married James Albert Mosher in Bangor. James was the son of Charles Henry Percy Mosher and Mary A. Crowell, also of the Bangor area. The couple settled in Bangor Ward 6, where they would remain throughout their married life. They had three daughters: Eleanor F. Mosher, who died in infancy; Mary Elizabeth Mosher, who would become Kevin's maternal grandmother; and Barbara Louise, who later married into the DeWolfe family.

James Albert Mosher died on April 26, 1945, one day before his 54th birthday, leaving Elsie a widow at age 50. Elsie survived her husband by more than two decades, living on in the Bangor area. She died on September 4, 1966, in Penobscot County at the age of 72. She was buried at Mount Hope Cemetery in Bangor, the second oldest garden cemetery in the United States, alongside her husband James.
