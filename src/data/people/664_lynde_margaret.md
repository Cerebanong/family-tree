## Family
- **Father:** Unknown

- **Spouse:** Thomas Lynde (m. 1632-11-27, All Hallows, Bread Street, London, England)

## Notes
Second wife of Deacon Thomas Lynde of Charlestown. Previously married Henry Jordan, Citizen and Cutler of London, who died before 15 May 1632. Married Thomas Lynde 27 Nov 1632 at All Hallows, Bread Street, London. Burial record at Charlestown gives age 62 years 6 months, so born ~Feb 1599/1600. Maiden name disputed: older sources (and the London burial record) give 'Martin' or 'Martine'; Robert Charles Anderson argues for 'Hudson' based on additional London records. Mother (with Thomas Lynde) of six children: John, Joseph, Sarah (Pierpont), Hannah (Trerice/Kelling), Samuel, Henry. FindAGrave #36821512; WikiTree Hudson-5341.

## Interesting Facts
Widow of a London cutler who married Deacon Thomas Lynde and emigrated to Massachusetts on the Griffin.
